# Revecs

**Agents trusted to act on your behalf** — answer for you, escalate for you,
transact for you.

Revecs builds reliable agent systems: accuracy-tested data oracles your agents
can buy per-call over [x402](https://x402.org), and AI assistants that answer
from real documents with citations — and hand off to a human rather than guess.

## Products

- **[Crypto ETF Sentinel](https://cryptoetfsentinel.com)** — the US spot
  crypto-ETF intelligence platform: pipeline tracking, outcome predictions and
  fund-health monitoring on 3,000+ curated SEC filings. Telegram bot, API, and
  product terminal.
- **[Biotech Catalyst Sentinel](https://biotechcatalystsentinel.com)** — accurate FDA PDUFA decision dates for
  US-listed biotechs, served per-call over x402.
- **Lettings Assistant** — an AI front desk that cites its sources and hands
  off to a human with the lead captured. *(demo available on request)*

Product cores are proprietary; the open-source x402 tooling below is how we
build and verify them. Milestones close on evidence — a working moment on a
live URL, not a code review.
